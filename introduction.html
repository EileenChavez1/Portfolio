Welcome!

My name is Eileen Hernandez Chavez, and this portfolio website is part of a creative web project I’m building to showcase who I am, what I enjoy, and what I’m learning.

I created this site to not only complete a course assignment, but also to give myself a space to collect my interests, hobbies, and future work. Web design is a new skill I’m exploring, and this project lets me practice design, structure, and code all in one place.

Quick Facts About Me

  • I love spending time outdoors, especially hiking on quiet trails.
  • I enjoy cooking new recipes and learning traditional ones from my culture.
  • I create pottery in my free time — it’s hands-on, meditative, and messy (in the best way).
  • I like playing video games that let me build, create, or solve problems.
  • I’m always curious and love learning — whether it's new tech or ancient techniques.
